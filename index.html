    Bocchi the Rock! is a band comedy 4-koma manga series written and illustrated by Aki Hamazi. The manga series serialized in Manga Time Kirara MAX by Houbunsha since February 2018 issue and collected five volumes in tank≈çbon format as of November 2022, and has an official translation by Yen Press, which will debut on October 17, 2023. It became a popular series and ranked 8th place in the Next Manga Awards 2019. An anime series produced by CloverWorks and premiered from October 9 to December 25, 2022, and has a theater compilation that will be screened in spring 2024.
